Baltimore High School Soccer Tournament

Purpose:

Welcome to the Baltimore High School Soccer Tournament website — your all-in-one destination for everything related to this year’s competition. Here, you’ll find detailed information about participating teams, game schedules, match results, and tournament updates. Whether you’re a player, parent, student, or supporter, this site keeps you connected with the action while also providing opportunities to donate and support the tournament’s success.

What to Expect

  • 8 Competing Schools: Top high school soccer programs from across Baltimore face off in a single-elimination bracket
  • Live Updates: Follow every match result and bracket advancement in real time right here on the site
  • Community & Support: Cheer on your school, connect with other fans, and help support the tournament through donations
